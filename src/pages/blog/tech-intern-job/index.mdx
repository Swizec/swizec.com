Here&#x27;s the deal, I&#x27;m a solopreneur working nights and weekends on this _Turn programmers into engineers_ business and I have more ideas than time. So I&#x27;m looking for an intern to help me out.





Why an intern? Three reasons:



1. I don&#x27;t think there&#x27;s enough work for an experienced software engineer
2. I think the tasks wouldn&#x27;t be challenging enough for an experienced engineer
3. Cost; this is a small bootstrapped business



## What I&#x27;m offering



I can&#x27;t promise you piles of money and I can&#x27;t say you&#x27;re going to build software used by millions. Such is life :)



What I _can_ offer is a paid learning opportunity.



You&#x27;ll work remotely and part-time to implement various solutions big and small in JavaScript, React, and associated web technologies. We&#x27;ll start small and go bigger as time goes on.



I don&#x27;t care where in the world you are, as long as your English is good enough that we can understand each other smoothly.



When you hit a snag, I&#x27;m here to help. If you need to buy a book or a course to learn something, I will buy it for you.



Think of this as a way to practice solving real problems. You are encouraged to leverage this experience into finding a real real job.



## Your job



Your job will be to implement things I don&#x27;t have time for, so I can focus on producing content. This will involve:



- improvements to swizec.com
- building features for learnwhileyoupoop.com
- evaluating opensource libraries
- building example code
- build and improve landing pages



The tech stack is a combination of React and Wordpress. You&#x27;re encouraged to leverage existing solutions instead of reinventing the wheel.



## How we&#x27;ll work together



We&#x27;re going to communicate primarily through Slack. Video calls when necessary.



We&#x27;re going to have daily checkins and generally keep in touch while working together. You are encouraged to reach out to me whenever.



You&#x27;ll be paid via PayPal or TransferWise every month based on an hourly rate we&#x27;ll figure out together. If neither PayPal nor TransferWise work for you, we can figure something out.



## How we&#x27;ll find out if we&#x27;re a fit



For hiring, I&#x27;m prioritizing [slope instead of y-intercept](http://qr.ae/TUphry). That is to say, I want you to be a fast learner not someone who already knows a lot.



You should have basic knowledge of JavaScript, React, HTML, etc. I&#x27;ll give you the chance to learn the rest.
