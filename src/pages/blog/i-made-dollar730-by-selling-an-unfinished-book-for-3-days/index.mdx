Saturday afternoon I decided I was ready to unleash [my book](https://leanpub.com/nightowls) on the world. Or rather, the 0.1 extremely unfinished draft version of the book.

> How finished do you consider it ? I'm counting 16 pages of actual content. I'm expecting a book to be a bit more work than a long blog post. /../ I understand that you would like to have money early to fund the process, but releasing drafts is not a good idea. And this \\\$3.14 [price point](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Price_point "Price point") is not serious. _~ A [Reddit](http://reddit.com/ "Reddit") commenter_
>
> Why is the price capped at \\\$10? I'm sure there are people who'd be willing to pay more _~ A HN commenter_
